Project Details

Summary

In this project, visual-inertial simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM) is implemented to localize the vehicle and perform landmark mapping of the environment using the extended Kalman filter (EKF) given that the measurements from the vehicle's inertial measurement unit (IMU) and the stereo camera. The map data was acquired around the University of Pennsylvania and provided by Professor Nikolay A. Atanasov.
