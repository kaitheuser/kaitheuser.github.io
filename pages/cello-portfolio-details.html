Analysis Report

Summary

In this project, the geometry, dimensions, boundary conditions, and symmetry of the cello with f-hole surface were defined using SolidWorks. The normal stresses in X and Y direction, von-Mises stress, and the deflection of the cello in Z-direction are determined using structural analysis after the loading of the bridge on the top face of the cello was applied. Another structural analysis are determined by using a full cello top face model with a smaller surface thickness. Moreover, the first 6 natural frequencies of the cello are determined and analyzed by using modal analysis that modeled the full cello top face and using another model analysis that used symmetry and modeled only half of the cello.
